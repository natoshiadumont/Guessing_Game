Guessing Game

RULES:

1. Guess any number between 1 and 100 then type your guess below. Press the crystal ball to submit prediction.

2. Using the hints provided, continue guessing until you get the right number.

3. You only have five chances to guess the correct number, or you will have to start over.

4.You may reset the game at any point!

5. GOOD LUCK AND HAVE FUN!
